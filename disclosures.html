IRS Circular 230

Federal tax advice disclosure

Any tax advice provided by ASTA is not intended or written to be used, and cannot be used, for avoiding penalties under the Internal Revenue Code or for promoting, marketing, or recommending to another party any transaction or matter addressed herein.

Not the IRS

ASTA is a private tax preparation service

  • ASTA is not affiliated with or endorsed by the IRS.
  • IRS acceptance is subject to IRS systems and rules.
  • Filing deadlines and requirements are governed by the IRS.

Estimates and outcomes

Refund estimates are preliminary and depend on information provided. Final refunds, balances, and processing timelines are determined by the IRS.

  • Refund estimates are not guarantees.
  • IRS adjustments may change outcomes.
  • Additional documentation may be required.

E-file authorization

Authorization requirements

ASTA requires a signed Form 8879 before transmitting an IRS e-file return.
