IRS IP PIN required for filing

The IRS Identity Protection PIN (IP PIN) is a 6-digit code issued annually. Find it in your IRS Online Account and provide it here to complete filing.

  1. Visit irs.gov/ippin
  2. Sign in or create an IRS Online Account
  3. Select "Get an IP PIN" and copy the 6-digit code for this year
