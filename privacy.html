Privacy policy

Protecting taxpayer data is a core obligation.

This policy explains how ASTA collects, uses, and safeguards personal information for tax preparation services.

Effective date

January 15, 2026

Updates will be posted on this page as policies evolve.

Information we collect

Data needed for tax preparation and filing

Identity information

Name, SSN, date of birth, and IRS IP PIN for verification and filing.

Tax documents

W-2s, 1099s, and supporting records you upload to the secure vault.

Contact details

Email, phone, and mailing information used for communication and delivery.

Account metadata

Login events, upload timestamps, and audit records for compliance.

How we use data

Purpose-limited and IRS-aligned

  • Prepare and transmit federal and state tax returns.
  • Verify taxpayer identity and prevent fraud.
  • Maintain audit trails required for compliance.
  • Communicate filing status and required documents.

Data sharing

We share data only with authorized tax professionals, IRS-authorized e-file providers, and service vendors required to deliver services.

  • No sale of personal information.
  • Vendor access limited by contract and scope.
  • Access limited to assigned preparers.

Retention & security

Safeguards designed for sensitive tax data

Retention

Records are retained for 7 years or as required by law or firm policy.

Encryption

Data is encrypted in transit and at rest in segmented storage.

Access controls

Role-based access and logging limit exposure and enable review.

Client rights

Data access, export, and deletion requests can be submitted to ASTA support.

For questions about this policy, contact privacy@atlassecuretax.com.
